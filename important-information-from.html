IMPORTANT INFORMATION FROM YOUR PRESIDENT | T.H.O.P.P.


The Townhomes of Paradise Park Owners Association, Inc

PO Box 033871



February 10, 2021

Dear Association Member,


The 2021 Annual Meeting of THOPP was held on January 30, 2021 and the draft Minutes of that meeting are attached. The 2021 Budget was adopted without changes. Copies were provided with the Annual Meeting Notice previously mailed. 

As previously reported the monthly maintenance fee was increased to $175.00 per month as of January 1, 2021. If major items like our roadway need attention a Special Assessment will be necessary and owners will be notified.

Your Board has elected the following to serve as Officers for 2021: Wanda Cordelli, President; Katie Mulholland, Vice President; and Donald Trafton, Secretary/Treasurer.

As included in the draft minutes there were a number of issues addressed by your Board. We are working hard to ensure the property is maintained properly with fiscal responsibility. 

If you have comments and/or concerns, please let one of your active Board members know.  

Thank you for your cooperation.


For the Board of Directors,


Sincerely,

Wanda Cordelli

Wanda Cordelli

THOPP President


Attachments: Draft Minutes of THOPP 2021 Annual Meeting
